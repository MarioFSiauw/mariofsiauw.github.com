2Days

Remember the Special Days

Initial Exploration for Client

Brief

2Days is a world calendar used to document special days in our lives that we will don't want to forget. Whether it's a birthday, wedding day, graduation, etc. Document the day on 2Days and always have the ease of access to it anytime. Events you have documented will be visible to other users. Go ahead and upload your special events and explore others day of happiness as well.

“My thought on the app is you open it up and immediately you see a scrollable list of events for the day throughout time in history that people own.”

Client

Ideal Process

Parker's main priority is to digitalize the parking pass experience. By doing so we will eliminate the need for blank stubs and toner. As someone who's constantly irritated by daily commute, I wanted Parker's experience to be as non-intrusive and low maintanence as possible.

Outcome

I wanted to narrow down the user journey as much as possible, presenting screens that are considered to be necessary. No additional steps and fluff required.

The Outcome

“I found myself throwing away a ridiculous amount of parking garage tickets”

Reflections and future plans

This is a fantasy piece. In order to bring this project to life it would need proper infrastructure binding with modern technologies used within the majority of parking garages across the world. Parker could lead to substantial savings from not having to buy physical materials that are dispensed in parking garages.
