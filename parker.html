Parker

A SIDE-PROJECT
ROLE: DESIGNER

Brief

The idea of Parker came to fruition when I found myself throwing away a ridiculous amount of parking garage tickets that I had stashed in my car's cupholder. Not only did I realize that I spend too much money on parking, but also the fact that so much paper is wasted with this mechanism. Parker is an idea that will help prevent this problem.

How will Parker solve the problem?

Parker's main priority is to digitalize the parking pass experience. By doing so we will eliminate the need for blank stubs and toner.
