Potluck

LED BY MATT MACNAUGHTON
A SIDE-PROJECT
ROLE: DESIGNER

-What is Potluck?

Potluck is a social shopping platform that empowers groups of people to play in challenge-based purchase experiences. Two parties are in play when you compete, a Pot Leader and Pot Contestants. Friends can decide amongst each other about which prize they will compete for and which challenge they will compete in.

-My Mission

I was approached by Matt to design the first rendition of Potluck. The main focus of this design process was to curate an accessable experience that is simple and entertainting to help effectively grow an early adopter audience.

-Problems with modern commerce

Since the recent increase in e-commerce engagement, the shopping experience has become an isolated experience. Consumers do not have the same opportunity to socialize around the shopping experience. Product discovery is increasingly becoming more dependent on algorithms instead of trusted social recommendations.

-Potluck is the solution

Potluck's mission is to revitalize the social aspect of shopping by combining e-commerce and mobile technology. We believe bringing social experiences can be achieved by focusing on these four specific topics:

Product Discovery

Potluck will drive new product discovery as a key feature of its service. Word of mouth products that friend groups value highly.

Challenge Based Experiences

Potluck plays a facilitator role for challenge based ways to purchase new products and "gift" these products to the victors. In principle, Potluck enables groups to consensually gift products to a challenge winner.

Group Payment

Potluck enables groups to split the cost of a single product and reduce entry costs of all participants. The group dynamics drive participation and help drive the price tag up for product choice to put real value on the line.

Social Shopping

Groups of friends will be introduced to new products through a gamified and challenge based environment. Friends can share in the perceived value of this product via the collective "buy in" that's part of the Potluck experience.
